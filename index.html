Portfolio Overview

To paraphrase Victor Hugo, there is nothing so powerful as an idea whose time has come. Over the past fifty years (UW Team, 2017), Data Science has grown over countless businesses and organizations world-wide. As my career progressed in a STEM field, I found that I was overwhelmed with acquisition, processing, and analysis of massive amounts of information and data. When I found data science and what could be done with it, I knew it could provide the tools the methodology I was seeking to harness large amounts of data and turn it into actionable information and insight. Throughout my studies in Applied Data Science at Syracuse through the School of Information Technology I have advanced my knowledge and applied many data science skills. Specifically, the skills demonstrated in this portfolio aims to capture some of those learnings in collection and organization of data, identification of patterns, statistical analysis, data mining, data strategy, discernment of actionable business insight and communication skills.
